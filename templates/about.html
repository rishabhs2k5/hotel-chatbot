About MyHotel

Established in 1995, MyHotel began as a small family-run retreat welcoming travelers with warmth and elegance. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in hospitality, blending modern comfort with timeless traditions.

Our mission has always been to create memorable stays — whether you’re here for business, leisure, or a special occasion. With carefully designed rooms, personalized services, and a passion for excellence, MyHotel continues to be a symbol of luxury and care.

Today, we proudly serve guests across multiple cities, offering not just a place to stay, but an experience to cherish.
